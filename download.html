LUCAN (Lucknow University Covid-19 Awareness Navigator)

LUCAN (Lucknow University Covid-19 Awareness Navigator) App is a mobile application, powered by SDI FoET LU, aimed to connect people and health services in our fight against COVID-19. LUCAN empowers the user by informing about potential risks of infections through WiFi tracing and equips people through self-assessment tools, health advices and helps to maintain the social distancing.
