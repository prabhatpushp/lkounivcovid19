Corona Virus Disease ( COVID-19 )

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a new virus. The disease causes respiratory illness (like the flu) with symptoms such as a cough, fever, and in more severe cases, difficulty breathing. You can protect yourself by washing your hands frequently, avoiding touching your face, and avoiding close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell.

Get in touch with your local NGO's and district administration to volunteer for this cause.

COVID-19 SYMPTOMS

  • cough
  • fever
  • Tiredness
  • Breathing Difficulty
  • Persistent Chest Pain
  • Sore Throat
  • diarrhoea

People may be sick with the virus for 1 to 14 days before developing symptoms.Older people, and people with other medical conditions (such as asthma, diabetes, or heart disease), may be more vulnerable to becoming severely ill. People with fever, cough or difficulty breathing should call their doctor and seek medical attention.

PREVENTION FROM COVID-19

  • Wash your hands regularly for 20 seconds, with soap and water or alcohol-based hand rub
  • Cover nose or mouth while sneezing and coughing
  • Stay home and Avoid close contact
  • self-isolate from others in the household if you feel unwell
  • Touch your eyes, nose, or mouth if your hands are not clean
