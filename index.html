Corona Virus Disease ( COVID-19 )

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious viral disease caused by a relatively new virus. The disease causes respiratory illness (just like the seasonal viral flu) with symptoms such as a cough, fever, and in more severe cases, difficulty in breathing. One can protect oneself by washing their hands frequently for at least 20 seconds, avoid touching your nose and face unnecessarily, and avoid coming in close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with a person who has tested positive.

Get in touch with your local NGO's and district administration to volunteer for this cause.

COVID-19 SYMPTOMS

  • cough
  • fever
  • Tiredness
  • Breathing Difficulty
  • Persistent Chest Pain
  • Sore Throat
  • diarrhoea

A person affected by Coronavirus may take 1 to 14 days before developing viral flu-like symptoms. Older people, and people with other medical conditions (such as asthma, diabetes, or heart disease), maybe more vulnerable to this virus and fall severely ill. People with fever, cough or difficulty in breathing should immediately contact their doctor and seek urgent medical attention.

PREVENTION FROM COVID-19

  • Wash your hands regularly for 20 seconds, with soap and water or alcohol-based hand rub.
  • Cover mouth and nose while coughing and sneezing.
  • Stay home and avoid close contact.
  • Self-isolate from others in the household if you feel unwell.
  • Do not touch your eyes, nose, or mouth if your hands are not clean.
